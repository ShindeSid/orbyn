Features

Everything you need to manage enterprise assets

From intake to retirement - the entire asset lifecycle, automated.

🔒

Conflict-free allocation

Every allocation is checked atomically before it's granted, the same way an OS won't hand a locked resource to two processes at once. No double-bookings, ever.

🔁

Queued transfers, not deadlocks

Want an asset someone else holds? File a transfer request instead of waiting indefinitely - approved by an Asset Manager or Department Head.

📅

Interval-scheduled bookings

Shared resources use the same overlap-detection logic an OS scheduler uses for time-sliced reservations - no two people can double-book a room or van.

🔧

Maintenance workflows

Requests flow through a 5-stage kanban - pending, approved, assigned, in progress, resolved - with asset status updating automatically at each step.

🔍

Audit cycles

Scope an audit to a department or location, verify every asset, and let Orbyn auto-flag missing or damaged items with a generated discrepancy report.

📊

Reports that matter

Utilization, maintenance frequency, retirement warnings, and warranty expiry - all in one dashboard, exportable to CSV whenever you need it.
